Brand introduction
Founded by Adolf Adi Dassler on August 18, 1949, in Herzogenaurach, Germany, adidas initially specialized in producing sports shoes. The brand name "adidas" is derived from the founder's nickname "Adi" and the first three letters of his surname "Das." After more than seven decades of development, it has become a leading global sportswear brand, covering sports shoes, apparel, accessories, and sports equipment, with profound influence in fields such as football, basketball, and running.

Brand philosophy
Pursuit of Excellence: Encourages people to continuously challenge themselves, break through limits, and pursue higher goals in both sports and life. By providing high-performance sportswear, it helps people achieve their best performance.
Spirit of Innovation: Dares to break traditions, continuously explores new design concepts, technologies, and materials, and integrates innovation into products to meet consumers’ evolving needs and lead the trends in sports fashion.
Individual Expression: Respects everyone’s individuality. Through a variety of styles, colors, and designs, consumers can choose clothing that suits them, showcasing their unique style and attitude, and using sportswear as a way to express their personality.
